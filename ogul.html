  • 7/25/2019 solo from the pavement near Point 5705 (approx. 6 miles one-way):
    • Followed FS Road 24N88, turned left at Point 5738, and continued on 24N88 to the intersection with 24N44.
    • Followed FS Road 24N44, turned left (north) at an intersection with a connector road.
    • Followed the connector to the intersection with 24N51XB.
    • Followed FS Road 24N51XB to the intersection with former FS Road 24N51XB2.
    • Headed east to the meadow at 39.9205°N 120.1054°W.
    • Followed an ATV trail southeast to a saddle at approx. 39.91842°N 120.10367°W.
    • Headed south off-trail to the saddle southwest of the summits at approx. 39.9095°N 120.1017°W.
    • Headed northeast to the saddle between the west and east summits.
    • First climbed the west summit which has the benchmark.
      Didn't stay long because of an ominous-looking cloud overhead.
      Briefly looked for but didn't find a register.
    • Then climbed the east summit which had a register (under a small cairn)
      placed by Bob McLaughlin on 6/22/2016.
  • Note from LoJ: "East Summit determined higher than west by 2 feet using photo pixel analysis."

3.2 English Mountain Tahoe National Forest 8,373
8373' (NGVD 29) USGS 7.5' Quad (1:24,000) "English Mountain, CA" (2000/2003)
 Class 2 1,273 SP   BB LoJ Pb PY WX 5/29/2018 with Angie
  • 5/29/2018 with Angie
    • Parked at the Visitor Parking sign in front of the closed Jackson Meadows Station.
    • Hiked via dirt roads to the outlet of Catfish Lake and crossed the outlet creek.
    • Hiked and bushwhacked cross-country to the north gully of English Mountain.
    • Climbed the north gully (class 2+) to a point on the summit ridge at around 8000 feet.
    • Contoured around to a point northwest of the summit at around 8100 feet.
    • Climbed up to the summit ridge (class 2+) and followed it a short way southeast to the summit.
    • Descended the west slope through the forest and two boulder fields to the 4WD road shown on the topo.
    • Followed the 4WD road to where it meets the road between Bowman Lake and Jackson Meadows Reservoir.
    • Followed the road to Jackson Meadows Reservoir back to where we parked.
