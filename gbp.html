  • Note from the DPS Peaks List (29th Edition, April 2003):
    The summit is higher than, and about 0.6 mi SSE of, the point labeled "Glass Mountain" on the topo.
  • About 1 hour and 20 minutes from Bishop to the trailhead [37.77393 N 118.68385 W]
  • 6 North to Benton [W], 120 West, pass Benton Hot Springs [W], left on Black Canyon Road, right on Sawmill Meadows Road
  • About 20-30 minutes from the beginning of Black Canyon Road to the trailhead (via gravel/dirt road)
  • About 1 hour and 10 minutes from the trailhead to the summit
  • Also hiked to the point labeled Glass Mountain (11,123') on the topo, aka Glass Mountain-North Peak [BB, LoJ, Pb]
